# Image Requirements for Didee Research Services

## Required Images for Beautiful Design:

### Homepage Images:
1. **research-hero.jpg** - Professional research team or modern office setting
2. **research-team.jpg** - Diverse team of researchers working together
3. **research-cta.jpg** - Modern research facility or data visualization

### Services Page Images:
4. **custom-research.jpg** - Business meeting or research presentation
5. **academic-writing.jpg** - Student or researcher writing/studying
6. **data-analysis.jpg** - Charts, graphs, or data visualization screens

### Recommended Image Sizes:
- Hero images: 1920x1080px or larger
- Service images: 800x600px
- Background images: 1200x800px

### Image Style:
- Professional, modern, clean
- High quality stock photos
- Consistent color scheme (blues, greens, professional tones)
- People should look diverse and professional

### Where to Place Images:
Save all images in: `public/images/` folder

The website will automatically use these images once placed in the correct location.
